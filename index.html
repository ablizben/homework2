About Me

Hello there! My name is Abby Benjamin and I'm a developer-in-training. I created this site as part of a project in my full-stack web development boot camp. My path to coding hasn't been exactly a straight one, as I started in the psychology research field and then made my way into human resources. After helping maintain an HRIS database for my current job at a data marketing agency, I found a love of coding and I haven't looked back since.

Aside from web development and programming, I enjoy boxing, running, learning new non-programming languages, and anything related to the horror genre. Please, stay a while and take a look around!
